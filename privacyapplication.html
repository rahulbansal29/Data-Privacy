Privacy Applications

  • Secure Messaging: End-to-end encryption in apps like Signal, WhatsApp, Telegram ensures private conversations.
  • Cloud Security: Data is encrypted both at rest and in transit to prevent breaches.
  • Healthcare Systems: Protecting patient records and sensitive medical information.
  • Financial Systems: Ensuring confidentiality of banking and payment details.
  • Homomorphic Encryption: Performing computations on encrypted data without exposing the underlying values.
  • Differential Privacy: Preserving user privacy while analyzing large datasets (e.g., AI/ML, government statistics).
